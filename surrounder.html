Surround·er

SCAVENGER HUNT MOBILE GAME

Surround·er is a scavenger hunt game, that helps bring people together in new environments while showing them fun and interesting facts and useful information about their new school and city.

OVERVIEW

  • ROLE:
    UX/UI Designer
  • OCCASION:
    School Project
  • DURATION:
    July 2019 - MAy 2020 (10 months)
  • METHOD:
    Design Thinking and Iterative Design
  • HOW MIGHT WE...
    Using more interesting way to engage new students and make information and social event more approachable?
  • KEY FEATURES:
    School-base treasure hunt, Avatar Creator

WRAP UP

It’s my first time using UX and design thinking methods to approach the problem, it’s definitely a learning progress for me as I went through the whole process.

I really enjoyed using pen and paper to sketch the wireframes, especially refining them after talking to the user. Because at that point, I knew that the product is getting better, and I would have more confidence that the product will meet users’ needs.

I would say the most interesting thing I learned from this project is that users won’t always know what they want, so I believe as a UX designer, it is important to find out what users are trying to achieve in a particular circumstance and knowing User's 'Jobs to Be Done'.
