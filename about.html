Tzu-Ling Tseng

UX/UI DESIGNER

Hello, I’m Tzu-Ling Tseng. A UX designer with a background in electrical and electronic engineering office administration.

As a personal assistant, I understand the importance of managing the overall workflow. I make sure the procedure is executed well based on the defined decision we made upon the meeting, and keep it flexible for any further changes according to the feedback from the user and colleagues during the process.

I also take accountability to balance the needs across multiple teams(contractors, clients, users, suppliers, and engineers), collecting data and conditions from the user’s side and pass it back to the office.

The same principles apply when it comes to designing a digital product. To ensure we have a better product that people enjoy using, I focus on the user’s need, staying flexible on the planning and implementing points of view, and continuously iterating through the designing process.

Additionally, volunteering at homeless shelters in the UK makes me a better communicator in a multicultural environment. As part of my daily role, I take the responsibility to hand over and pass the works and messages to the staff and other volunteers. It’s crucial to understand the different communication styles across cultures, different ways of thinking, and how to provide feedback effectively.

My goal is to turn my passion into my profession by working as a UX designer, using my skills and knowledge to offer companies different perspectives across the team.
